# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

The Go Gym is a learning space. We want it to be welcoming, harassment-free, and encouraging for everyone
— especially beginners and people who've bounced off Go before. Learning is hard enough; we don't make it
harder for each other.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind, patient, and assume good faith — remember everyone here is learning.
- Give feedback on code and prose, not on people.
- Respect differing experience levels; "obvious" questions are welcome.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language of any kind.
- Demeaning someone for not knowing something.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and comments that violate this code, and may block
repeat offenders. To report a concern, contact **benbald21@gmail.com**. Reports are handled discreetly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted in spirit from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
